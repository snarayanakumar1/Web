How to make Microwave Chocolate Chip Cookie



Preperation

1. Add 2 tbsp butter, 1 tbsp brown sugar, 1 tbsp sugar, ¼ tsp baking powder, ⅛ tsp salt and ¼ tsp vanilla in a bowl and mix
2. Next, add 3 tbsp flour and 2 tbsp chocolate chips and mix thoroughly. Transfer the mix to a microwave-friendly bowl and heat for 60 seconds. Top with ice cream.
3. Enjoy!

Ingredients

2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon sugar
¼ teaspoon baking powder
⅛ teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
3 tablespoons flour
2 tablespoons chocolate chips
½ cup ice cream, optional
